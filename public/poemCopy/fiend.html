Now you have a voice you are a terror
of demands, and with no lips to form words
I have no choice but to learn an idiolect
of slow blinks and shaken tails. This meowl
is for biscuits, that one for brushing
and a chirrup to tell me to stop and watch
as you stretch on the sill, one eye lidded
as the other waits for entertainments
from the toys that visit the seed block.
