(After that most disruptive monk)

You can't go far wrong
with a decent recipe
for pot roast puppy

A while later the student lifted his head. "It is a syllabic skeleton of a haiku; a starved sickness of form. It is a performance, a theft of bones to put on a stage. It is a download exercise that counts only things. It is a waste of tongues."

The master said nothing, moved nothing beyond a dip of a spoon into the depths of the stew.
