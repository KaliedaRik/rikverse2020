I met you first on the back of a boot boy,
shaved skull and dirty mouth, defender

of a homeland born in chants on terraces.
An old soldier's signal left in the dole queue

when brighter banners were hoisted high.
The gutter shills have your history wrong –

the dragons you slayed were penny-scraping
and moat-digging; hates replaced by hugs

even for the skinhead who believed the lies
whispered in stadiums: England expects &hellips;
