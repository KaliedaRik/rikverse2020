Half ten ay em: I am surrounded
by three suits and a power skirt.
Hands are smoothed to hair and mouths,
fingers count points and derelictions,
jaws are moved rapidly,

unsure of direction, but smother
insecurity from the gaze of open-plan officers:
panic is demonstrated cordially,
movements restrained, tone near-even

and I am no longer centre-staged:
innocence is secured,
a reputation is sweet.

I didn't lose their fucking files!
