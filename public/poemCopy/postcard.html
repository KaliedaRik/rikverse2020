Molyvos must be
the essence of Greece.
We're living in a house
on a cobbled street
with steps, but no cars –
him next door parks
a donkey in his
dry stonewall garage.

We even have a view
of the bay, complete
with picturesque island
and fishermen in old boats
teasing the sea for octopus –
which then hang in tavernas
to dry on nylon
washing lines, tempting wasps.
