You laugh like a gutter – a whoop
of hollow gulps slung from lungs
to pulp even the spider cowed
in her mat-thick webs in the cornice.

Every bottle you meet is a challenge
to be accepted and defeated;
your claim to care only for vintage
belches through the torrents consumed.

The way you stroke me after sex –
a toddler pets its kitten more sweetly …
Open your eyes wide, and wide again:
our time together will end with a grave!
