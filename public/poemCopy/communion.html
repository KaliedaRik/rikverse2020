For my first burlesque in the warm May sun
I seek an audience of crows; there is much
for me to expose and, when done, they caw
an appreciation for my leisure-spun girths.

I libate with oils, wheel onto my back
and let Aten's fingers massage me: I see
you've fattened well, little calf, the lost god
whispers; I accept this singe of a gift.
